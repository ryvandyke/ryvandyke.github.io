This portfolio

showcases my design process and documents my artistic choices in the areas of scenic and props designs. The three musical stage productions I will use to as examples in my portfolio are: The Drowsy Chaperone, Big Fish, and Anything Goes.

Objectives and Goals:

1. To showcase and describe my designs work in the technical theatre areas of scenic and props design.

2. To provide pre/post-show design examples for my Stagecraft students to use during class.

3. To archive document my work and evaluate my growth as an artist over time.

4. To provide insight into my design material choices based on limitations and other factors throughout the design process. .
