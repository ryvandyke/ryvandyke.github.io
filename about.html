About Me

My name is Ryan VanDyke. I currently live in Liberty, Missouri, with my wife and daughter. I have been a high school educator for 21 years, teaching theatre and film. I completed my undergraduate degree at Baker University in Baldwin City, Kansas, in 1998 with a degree in education and theatre.

This summer, I began my Master's program and will graduate in the Spring of 2023.
My long-term goals include teaching at the high school level and expanding my professional design resume by working with local theatre companies during the summer.
